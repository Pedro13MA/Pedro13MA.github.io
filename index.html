Features

Built for accuracy, not volume

Every component is designed to prefer missing a deal over publishing garbage.

01

Multi-store price monitoring

Parallel async scraping across retailers with adaptive polling, HTTP caching and anti-ban resilience.

02

Real deal detection

Conservative gates filter fake discounts, inflated original prices, and implausible price drops.

03

Amazon product enrichment

Search pages discover ASINs; product pages (/dp/{ASIN}) enrich seller, FBA and condition metadata.

04

Smart scoring & filtering

Confidence tiers, priority engine and cross-store deduplication keep signal high and noise low.

05

Telegram alert system

Topic-based group publishing plus private /alertar subscriptions for products you care about.

+

Production lean

Single Python process · SQLite WAL · runs on a €4/month VPS with zero Redis, Docker or Postgres overhead.
